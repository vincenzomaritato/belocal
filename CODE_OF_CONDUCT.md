# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We want this project to be a respectful, professional, and technically rigorous place to collaborate. Contributors are expected to communicate clearly, disagree constructively, and focus criticism on the work rather than the person.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in technical discussions.
- Assume good intent, but challenge weak reasoning with evidence.
- Keep reviews factual, specific, and actionable.
- Avoid harassment, hostility, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Respect privacy and do not share credentials, personal data, or private communications.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Insults, intimidation, or repeated hostile behavior
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention
- Publishing private information without permission
- Deliberate disruption of project discussions or review processes

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and participation that violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

Report serious incidents privately to the maintainers through the security/contact channel defined in [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md).
